About Us

We Protect & Serve Boulder Creek Neighborhood

We are here to make sure the citizens of Boulder Creek Neighborhood have the campfires, hockey rink and general quality of life as possible.

We were formed in 2020 during the first months of the pandemic as a way to get together ourside and walk the neighborhood.

Since then, we have evolved and grown into a group of service minded individuals serving the community.

Our missing is to make the life of all people in the Boulder Creek neighborhood the best it can be.

We want a neighborhood that has the best facilities, highest quality amenities and best quality of life for all to enjoy.
